Description

The location formerly housing Moda Handbags is a vintage style multipurpose art deco building on the corner of 5th ave & NW 24th st. Built in the 1950s, this building still has it’s original window fixtures & building features creating a throwback vibe any tenant would be thrilled to capitalize on. The space can be divided into 4 partitions, allowing a variety of businesses access, fostering an ecosystem of closely collaborating companies, or can be leased in its entirety, facilitating a large scale operation with it’s spacious interior.

The location is on the same block as the former RC Kola factory and China Girl, making it part of the anchor of the emerging 5th ave fashion district in the Wynwood Arts District. Surrounded by murals and street art, wide sidewalks, and a tree lined median, this location is prime for the coming influx of people and businesses. Conveniently close to The Lab and many other business, this location is ideal for “getting business done” with the creative class currently growing in Wynwood. The tenant that takes interest in this property understands the value of creating foot traffic on what will be Wynwood’s greatest block.
