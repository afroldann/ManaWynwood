MANAWYNWOOD
PRODUCTION VILLAGE
[ MWPV ]

ManaWynwood Production Village (MWPV) is a multipurpose production studio and raw space equipped to facilitate all industry needs. Our gated compound, with on-site security, sits on 6 acres of prime real estate in the heart of Miami's Wynwood Art District.

The studio's main building is a 36,000-square-foot sound stage, along with it’s 100,000 square feet of raw space, and 400 parking spaces. Adjacent to our studio is the ManaWynwood office space, and also a 6,700 square-foot warehouse facility with an additional 4 acre lot/staging area.

MWPV accommodates large scale productions from feature films and television shows to events and trade shows, doubling as a convention center or concert venue, hosting art openings and fashion shows​.

Conveniently located, it operates within the only free trade zone in the area, making it an ideal place for international art distributors and vendors. It's proximity to the Miami International Airport, Interstate Highway 95 and all of Miami's most culturally influential neighborhoods (Downtown Miami, the Design District, Miami Beach), makes MWPV the most practical and desirable location for a variety of events.

At MWPV our main focus is to nurture Miami's cultural scene through quality and innovation. With our ability to play host to major film productions and other influential affairs, our vision is to become one of Miami’s most prominent hubs for the arts and culture, and to continue to bring international recognition to the humble streets of the Wynwood Art District.

MANAWYNWOOD
RC COLA FACTORY

The former RC Kola factory has been a long time monument to the graffiti and street art culture that has embodied the look & attitude of the Wynwood Arts District. With direct exposure to I-95, having over 100k vehicles pass by daily, the once abandoned factory has received new life as a concert venue, event space, and production space. Catering to the needs of the EDM community, film production crews, and artists from around the world, the space continues to maintain the gritty feel of an open air graffiti monument.
