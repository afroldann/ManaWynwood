ART WALK

Wynwood has experienced unprecedented growth over the past few years, part of its success is owed to the continued community support of the monthly Art Walk, with thousands of people to walk the streets, browsing the galleries, and taking in all our district has to offer. Whether you’re interested in the party, food, art, or just a chance to spend time with friends, Wynwood’s monthly second Saturday Art Walk has proven to be a permanent fixture of what to expect from the neighborhood.

MANAWYNWOOD
ART BASEL WEEK

Art Basel has become an annual staple of the Wynwood Art District, every year growing bigger & badder, creating an energy our city has never experienced before. After enjoying huge success from events held on our properties for Art Basel 2013, including art installations by Wynwood Embassy and Flaunt Magazine, and events with Red Bull Radio, Kendrick Lamar, Quest Love, Trick Daddy, and Boy George, among others, Mana Wynwood is committed to the continued support of the arts community, with plans to host annual rounds of the best events for the season.

This year our sister company, Mana Contemporary, a leading arts destination and home to a vibrant creative community, will be curating art exhibits & installations as part of our annual festivities for Art Basel Week. Opened in 2011 in Jersey City, Mana Contemporary is one of the largest and most innovative contemporary art organizations in the United States. Through vertically-integrated design, Mana provides art services, spaces, and programming for artists, collectors, curators, performers, students, and the greater community within a single location.
